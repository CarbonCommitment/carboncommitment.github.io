Campus Carbon Commitment Collective

Campus Carbon Commitment Collective (CCCC) is a student non-profit organization headquartered in Beijing. We are on the road to help achieve net zero greenhouse gas emissions across secondary schools in China.

Read Our White Paper

Our Mission

China has just set its 2060 Carbon Neutrality Target. How China’s carbon neutrality goal is defined and achieved will impact global success in meeting the 1.5C warming target. It is our belief that the first chapter of our climate positive future is about educating the next-generation leaders in China through building a carbon-neutral campus in across all secondary schools in China.

The CCCC is achieving this goal through our 3-step strategy: 1) raise awareness, 2) conduct research, and 3) provide guidance to make sure all member schools will make impactful yet actionable plans to reduce carbon emission.

Raise Awareness

Protecting the environment is important, but where and how do we, as teenagers in secondary schools, contribute to it and why do our efforts matter. CCCC educates the next-gen leaders in China through a wide range of events and scholarships.

Conduct Research

We believe that "if you can't measure it, you can't manage it" also applies in carbon emission control and have set the plan to measure the status and impacts of carbon footprints in major secondary schools.

Provide Guidance

Achieving a carbon-neutral campus is not an easy task - it involves different aspects and could be a decade-long task. We are providing the strategies and tools necessary to get started on this mission.

Our Structure

Scholarship Committee

The Scholarship Committee is responsible for raising donations, selecting candidates for our annual scholarship, and issuing the award.

Research Committee

The Research Committee conducts carbon footprint research  and publishes our Carbon Commitment White Paper.

Operations Department

The Operations Department manages day-to-day operations, including partnership, marketing, and collective member recruiting.

Our Initiatives

Climate Change Scholarship

We have an annual award for the best student-led sustainability project, with a ¥10,000 CNY scholarship. Nomination opens in each July and candidates will be selected with partner organizations across China.

Carbon Research Fellows

The Carbon Research Fellows project is a research-based program for students in secondary schools in China to analyze carbon impacts at their school. We have professors from top universities as academic advisors.

Carbon Commitment White Paper

We are issuing an annual white paper to analyze the current state of carbon footprints on campus in China and give out the most up-to-date toolbox and strategies to help our member schools achieve carbon neutrality.
